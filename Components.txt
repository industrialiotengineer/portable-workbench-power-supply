🔧 Components List


🔋 Power & Battery Section
18650 Lithium-ion Batteries – 4 pcs (4S pack)
→ Series connection gives ~14.8V nominal (16.8V full). Backbone of the system.
4S BMS (Battery Management System) – 1 pc
→ Protects from overcharge, over-discharge, short circuit, and balances cells.
⚡ Input / Charging Section
12V DC Female Connector –1
→ External power input (adapter/charger). 
10A Fuse + Holder – 1 set
→ First line of defense. Saves your circuit if something goes wrong.
ON/OFF Switch – 2 pcs
→ Main power control and optional secondary control (like output enable).

🔄 Power Conversion Section
DC-DC Step-Up Module (6009 / 6019) – 1 pc
→ Boosts battery voltage to required output (e.g., stable 12V output).
DC-DC Step-Down Module (4015) – 1 pc
→ Reduces voltage for charging or controlled outputs.
10K Multi-turn Potentiometer – 1 pc
→ Fine adjustment of voltage (more precise than single-turn).
🔌 Output Section
12V DC Female Connector –1
→ External power OUTPUT . 
USB Female Connector – 1 pc
→ For 5V output (phones, small devices).
Banana Connectors (Red & Black) – 1 pair
→ Clean and strong output terminals for general use.
DC Female Connector – (from above, reused)
→ Can also serve as output port if needed.
💡 Indicators & Monitoring
5mm LEDs – 2 pcs (Red & Green)
→
🔴 Red: Charging indicator
🟢 Green: Power ON / Output active
1K Resistors – 2 pcs
→ Current limiting for LEDs (protects them from burning out).
7-Segment Voltmeter Display – 1 pc
→ Shows battery/output voltage in real time.
🔧 Miscellaneous
Connection Wires
→ Use proper thickness (important for current handling).
Storage Box / Enclosure – 1 pc
→ Keeps everything safe, organized, and professional-looking.
